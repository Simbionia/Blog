    Copyright and other constraints affecting websites

    Copyright protects the interests of writers, artists, musicians and other creative people and all the projects that they create. Copyright affects how businesses decide what to do in a lot of different ways on a regular basis. Images and text for business websites, advertisements and newsletters are all subject to copyright law. Selecting copyrighted materials for businesses without permission, such as lifting photographs from the Internet somewhere other than a royalty free site, which states that the images are free for the public to use, makes it so the business could be subject to a very expensive copyright infringement lawsuit. Employment contracts can also be affected by copyright law when businesses put in clauses in contracts specifying who owns the copyrights to any creative product produced at the workplace.

    If using copyrighted material with consent, then the websites may likely have to pay royalty fees as well incurring further expenses for them. This means that it's a much better idea to use self made or royalty free content from places such as ghetty images on your website so as to not rack up a much larger running cost than needed.

    Issues that people with disabilities may face when using webpages and how we overcome them

    There are many ways that people could struggle when it comes to accessing websites.

  • Hard for them to process/look at non-text based content

    • People with impaired vision or brain injuries may have problems with viewing images making it so images/video may appear blurry. Because of this it's important to make it so there is text based alternatives or descriptions for this content to make it so these users can more easily access the website.

  • Hard for them to process/look at text based content

    • The opposite of the previous problem, people with sight issues may also have trouble looking at text based content. To help these it people out it's a good idea to have ways to increase the size of the text to make it easier to make out, as well as having images and video options that also display the content from the page.

  • Issues with video

    • Another problem that could come up is that users who have hearing/sensory issues may have a lot of trouble with any video content on websites. For this it's a good idea to have captions or a description of what is in the video so that these users can use the sites with minimal problems.

  • Struggles with using computer hardware

    • Users may not use a mouse when interacting with the internet, so this means that it's important to make it so websites can be fully used with only a keyboard. Also some others may use voice input to interact with websites so having it so your websites can interface with these functions too is also important.

    Ways to improve productivity and efficiency

  • Prioritise what you have to do

    • When it comes to work make sure that you have the tasks that you need to do in the right order. By doing this you can make it so that the more important tasks that you have to do are the ones that are getting done first and that may help further down the line.

  • Improve your knowledge of your equipment

    • Knowing all the capabilities of the equipment that you're using can save a lot of time in the long run, this could be things such as knowing all the keyboard shortcuts that a programme uses which may speed things up considerably.

  • If needed, make sure to ask for help

    • When you're stuck it may seem like a point of pride to try and force yourself through without asking for help, this is could be a big problem though as who knows how long you could be stuck. It's a much simpler, and better, idea to just ask for help if it's going to seriously impact your work.

  • Take regular breaks

    • Working for an extended period of time on something thats important/nearly finished may seem tempting, but if you keep working for longer and longer your productivity will take a serious hit as your mind gets more fatigued. To stop this from happening take a 5 minute break every hour or so to refresh your mind and body.
